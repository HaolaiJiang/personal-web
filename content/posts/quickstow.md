Have you ever put something away "for safe keeping," only to completely forget where it is when you actually need it?

* "Where on earth did I put the Christmas ornaments from last year? I recall seeing them in July..."
* "Where is that landscaper's business card? The grass is getting very tall."

We've all been there. That’s why I built QuickStow—a simple, effective solution to track exactly where you put your stuff.

![QuickStow Comic](/quickstow-intro-comic.png)

### What is QuickStow? ###
QuickStow is a lightning-fast web app designed for one thing: saving you from your own memory. Whether it's seasonal gear, important documents, or random spare parts, QuickStow remembers so you don't have to.

### Why You'll Love It ###
* Clean & Simple UI: No clutter, no complicated menus. Just open the app, type what you’re storing and where, and you’re done in seconds.
* Flexible & Private: Start using it immediately without creating an account. Your data stays privately on your device. Want to sync across devices? One tap lets you sign in securely with Google.
* Secure Cloud Sync: When you do sign in, your data is encrypted and synced privately using Google Firebase. Strict security rules ensure only you can access your locations.
* Smart Search: Retrieve your items instantly with a powerful search bar that shows you exactly when and where you last saw them.
